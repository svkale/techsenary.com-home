Guidelines for Authors

1.             Prepare a manuscript as per the formatting guidelines.

2.             Select the suitable type of Manuscript (Review article/ research article / short communication.....)

3.             Research article may contain: Abstract, Introduction, Methodology, Experimentation, Results and Discussion, Conclusion, References.

4.             Along with manuscript, submit the report of plagiarism.

5.             While submission of manuscript, it is mandatory to submit the details of minimum two reviewers.

6.             Selection of reviewer is a sole decision of Editor of the journal.

7.             On the basis of suggestions from reviewer and plagiarism report, editor can reject the manuscript.

8.             Suggestions form reviewers will be sent to author.

9.             Authors have to resubmit the manuscript incorporating suggestions.

10.         On the acceptance of manuscript, authors have to submit signed copyright form and make an online payment.

11.         On successful publishing of manuscript, author will be notified with mail and will receive soft copy of Published paper.
