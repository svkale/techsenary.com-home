TechSenary Journal of Engineering ReSearch (TSJER)

Aim

TechSenary Journal of Engineering ReSearch (TSJER) is an International peer reviewed journal with a quarterly publication frequency. It focusses on advancement of research in varied disciplines of Engineering, Science and Technology.

Scope

TechSenary Journal of Engineering Research (TSJER ) accepts full length original research papers, reviews, case studies related to all areas of Engineering, Science and Technology Viz. Civil, Mechanical, Industrial, Electrical, Computer, Chemical, Petroleum, Aerospace, Architectural. TSJER is intended to serve a wide range of educationists, scientists, specialists, researchers and similar professionals in different engineering disciplines. Our target is to reach all universities, research centers and institutes in the globe and is planned to serve as an academic medium and an important reference on the advancement and dissemination of research results that support high level learning, teaching and research. Through its publications TSJER aims to increase the visibility of these scholarly subjects thereby promoting usage of the subjects and their impact on the professional community.

TechSenary Journal of Heat and Fluid Systems(TSJHFS)

Aim

TechSenary Journal of Heat and Fluid Systems(TSJHFS) is an International peer reviewed journal with a quarterly publication frequency.

Scope

TechSenary Journal of Heat and Fluid Systems (TSJHFS) accepts full length original research papers, reviews, case studies related to Heat Transfer devices and systems, fluid flow systems, Heat and Mass transfer, Refrigeration, Air Conditioning, Phase change, Thermal Energy Storage, Heat Pipes, Heat Pumps, Cryogenics, Ventilation, Heating, Space cooling, Energy related issues in production and occupied spaces. All allied applications of Heat and Fluid systems in various disciplines like Civil, Mechanical, Industrial, Electrical, Computer, Chemical, Petroleum, Aerospace, Architecture etc. The prime focus is to promote the experimental and computational studies pertaining to fundamental and applied research in listed domains.
Our target is to reach all universities, research centers and institutes in the globe and is planned to serve as an academic medium and an important reference on the advancement and dissemination of research results that support high level learning, teaching and research. Through its publications TSJHFS aims to increase the visibility of these scholarly subjects thereby promoting usage of the subjects and their impact on the professional community.
